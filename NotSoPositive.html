02 THE PROBLEMS

THE PROBLEMS

PLAGUING THE INDUSTRY

Advertisers are assaulted.
Media buyers can get kickbacks from ad platforms with their client's money and pocket the difference without anyone ever knowing. These ad rebates hurt advertisers while the middle man profits… billions of dollars a year.
Publishers are powerless.
At least 50% of all digital ad revenue is lost in the ad buying ecosystem before it reaches the publishers. Even though they control the audience everyone wants access to, the publishers’ hands are tied.
Ad networks fuel the fire.
Deceptive tactics such as click farms and ad stacking are burning billions in advertisers’ budgets. The middle men who control the ad buying process are the only ones with the data to audit it.
